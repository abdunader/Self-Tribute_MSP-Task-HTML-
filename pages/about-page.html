My Story

Early Life

I was born and raised in Maadi, where my passion for Computers began to develop. From an early age, I was fascinated by how computers and machines work, which later influenced my educational and career choices.

Education

My educational journey began at Al Fath Al Azhary elementary School, followed by Al maadi school for boys. Later, I pursued higher education at Helwan University, where I studied Computer Science.

During my academic years, I developed a strong foundation in Computers and programming that continues to guide my professional endeavors today.

Career Path

My professional career started at Vodafone, where I worked as a Customer Service representative. This experience taught me valuable lessons about Systems in giant companies like Vodafone.

Currently, I am Data Entry, where I'm focused on dealing with data and learning more about data science.

Personal Interests

Outside of my professional life, I enjoy reading, charity work, hiking. These activities help me maintain a balanced lifestyle and continue to inspire my creativity.
